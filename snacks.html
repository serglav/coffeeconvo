Coffee Snacks

Coffee shops almost always have good snacks to accompany the coffee. Some of the most popular snacks are listed below, enjoy!

Cookies

One of the most common types of snacks for coffee. Helps to get the full sugar rush experience! Some of the most common types of cookies are:

  • Chocolate Chip
  • Peanut butter Cookies
  • Sugar cookies

Muffins

A very popular coffee snack that is available in a majority of coffee shops. Calorie alert! Muffins can have more then 400 calories each, eat with care!

  • Plain Muffins
  • Blueberry Muffin
  • Oatmeal Muffin

Croissants

Most coffee shops also offer a variety of croissants. One of the most popular is a butter croissant!

  • Butter Croissant
  • Ham and Cheese Croissant (Great breakfast option)!
